SYNOPSIS

Joel and Ellie, a pair connected through the harshness of the world they live in, are forced to endure brutal circumstances and ruthless killers on a trek across a post-outbreak America.

Characters

Joel Miller The main character of the story, Joel is a grizzled survivor of a dangerous post pandemic world, who has slowly lost his sense of morality after the loss of his daughter. He lived his life in a community while attempting to get in contact with his brother Tommy. In a series of events, he eventually is tasked in keeping a girl named Ellie alive.
Ellie Orphaned since she was a baby, Ellie lived a life in a military camp before escaping. She later got bit by an infected. However, instead of usualy turning within 3 days, she hasn't turned into an infected for weeks. This is why Joel was tasked to protect her, as she can be the key to finding a vaccine and to hopefully stop the infection that has been ongoing for years.
